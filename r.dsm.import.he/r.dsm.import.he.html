DESCRIPTION

r.dsm.import.he downloads digital surface models (DSM, in German DOM) for Hessen (HE) and area of interest. The data "Digitales Oberflächenmodell" is downloaded from Hessische Verwaltung für Bodenmanagement und Geoinformation and can be used under the specification of the licence: HVBG, Datenlizenz Deutschland – Zero – Version 2.0, DOM1.

EXAMPLE

Hessen example

Download and import DSM with native resolution: 
r.dsm.import.he aoi=aoi output=dsm -r
