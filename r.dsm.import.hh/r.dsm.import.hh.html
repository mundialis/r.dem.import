DESCRIPTION

r.dsm.import.th downloads digital surface models (DSM, in German DOM) for Hamburg (HH) and area of interest. The data "Digitales Höhenmodell Hamburg" is downloaded from daten-hamburg and can be used under the specification of the licence: Freie und Hansestadt Hamburg, Landesbetrieb Geoinformation und Vermessung (LGV), Datenlizenz Deutschland – Namensnennung – Version 2.0, bDOM.

EXAMPLE

Hamburg example

Download and import DSM with native resolution: 
r.dsm.import.hh aoi=aoi output=dsm -r
