DESCRIPTION

r.dtm.import.hb downloads digital terrain models (DTM, in German DGM) for Bremen and Bremerhaven (HB) and area of interest. The data "Digitales Höhenmodell Bremen" is downloaded from METAVER and can be used under the specification of the licence: Landesamt GeoInformation Bremen, Creative Commons Namensnennung 4.0 International.

EXAMPLE

Bremen example

Download and import DTM with native resolution: 
r.dtm.import.hb aoi=aoi output=dtm -r
