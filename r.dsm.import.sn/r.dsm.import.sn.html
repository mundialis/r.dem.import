DESCRIPTION

r.dsm.import.sn downloads digital surface models (DSM, in German DOM) for Sachsen (SN) and area of interest. The data "Digitales Oberflächenmodell" is downloaded from Landesvermessung Sachsen and can be used under the specification of the licence: GeoSN, Datenlizenz Deutschland – Namensnennung – Version 2.0, DOM1.

EXAMPLE

Sachsen example

Download and import DSM with native resolution: 
r.dsm.import.sn aoi=aoi output=dsm -r
