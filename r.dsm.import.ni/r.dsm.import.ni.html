DESCRIPTION

r.dsm.import.ni downloads digital surface models (DSM, in German DOM) for Niedersachsen (NI) and area of interest. The data "Digitales Oberflächenmodell Niedersachsen" is downloaded from OpenGeoData.NI and can be used under the specification of the licence: © GeoBasis-DE/LGLN YEAR, Data modified (IF DATA MODIFIED), Creative Commons (CC-BY 4.0), for detailed information see terms of service.

EXAMPLE

Niedersachsen example

Download and import DSM with native resolution: 
r.dsm.import.ni aoi=aoi output=dsm -r
