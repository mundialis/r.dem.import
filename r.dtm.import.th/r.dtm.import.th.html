DESCRIPTION

r.dtm.import.th downloads digital terrain models (DTM, in German DGM) for Thüringen (TH) and area of interest. The data "Digitales Geländemodell (DGM)" is downloaded from Geoportal Thüringen and can be used under the specification of the licence: © GDI-Th, Datenlizenz Deutschland – Namensnennung – Version 2.0, DGM.

EXAMPLE

Thüringen example

Download and import DTM with native resolution: 
r.dtm.import.th aoi=aoi output=dtm -r
