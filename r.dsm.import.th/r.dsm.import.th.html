DESCRIPTION

r.dsm.import.th downloads digital surface models (DSM, in German DOM) for Thüringen (TH) and area of interest. The data "Digitales Oberflächenmodell" is downloaded from Geoportal Thüringen and can be used under the specification of the licence: © GDI-Th, Datenlizenz Deutschland – Namensnennung – Version 2.0, DOM.

EXAMPLE

Thüringen example

Download and import DSM with native resolution: 
r.dsm.import.th aoi=aoi output=dsm -r
